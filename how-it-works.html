How It Works

Learn how to use the WC 2026 Predictor

Overview: WC 2026 Predictor allows fans to predict every FIFA World Cup 2026 match, build a complete knockout bracket, and explore different tournament outcomes.

1. Predict Group Stage Matches

Start by entering your predicted score for every group-stage match. The predictor automatically updates group standings, points, goal difference, and qualified teams based on your predictions.

2. Follow Automatic Qualification

As you complete the group stage, teams automatically advance into the knockout bracket according to FIFA World Cup 2026 qualification rules.

3. Build Your Knockout Bracket
  • Round of 32
  • Round of 16
  • Quarter-finals
  • Semi-finals
  • Third-place Match
  • Final
  • World Cup Champion

Predict every knockout match and decide who will lift the FIFA World Cup trophy.

4. Explore Tournament Analytics

View projected group winners, qualification paths, finalists, and tournament champions based on your predictions.

5. Save Your Progress

Predictions are stored locally in your browser, allowing you to return later and continue where you left off without creating an account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. You can update predictions at any time before finalising them.
