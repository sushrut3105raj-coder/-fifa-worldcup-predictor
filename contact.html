Frequently Asked Questions

Is WC 2026 Predictor free to use? +
Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no premium tier, no hidden costs. It always will be.
Are my predictions saved? +
Your predictions are saved to your browser's localStorage when you click "Save". They'll persist between sessions on the same device and browser. Clearing your browser data will reset them.
Why did my bracket reset? +
If we update the bracket structure to fix a bug (as we did with the R32 allocation), old bracket data may be cleared for accuracy. Your group stage scores are always preserved.
Is this affiliated with FIFA? +
No. WC 2026 Predictor is an independent, fan-made project. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to FIFA or any national football federation.
Can I use this for betting? +
No. This platform is strictly for entertainment purposes only and must not be used in connection with gambling, sports betting, or any wagering activity.
The standings for my group look wrong — why? +
Standings update based on the scores you enter. Make sure all 6 matches in the group have scores filled in. Tiebreakers are resolved by: Points → Goal Difference → Goals Scored → FIFA Ranking.
